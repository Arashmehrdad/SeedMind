# SeedMind Week 14 Demonstration Script

Declared duration: 166 seconds

## Scene 1 — Authority and scope (18 seconds)

SeedMind Week 14 is a packaging layer over committed Week 8–13 evidence. It adds no new cognitive capability. The only production action authority remains the Week 12 rollback checkpoint, with the general controller active and the rejected specialist inactive.

## Scene 2 — Reusable skill and contribution (24 seconds)

Week 8 compiled a reusable symbolic approach-and-push skill and reused it without rediscovery. Week 9 then demonstrated bounded contribution: ten successful attempts out of twelve, grounded outcome verification, honest failure handling, and support reduction only after repeated competence.

## Scene 3 — Ambition and apprenticeship (24 seconds)

Week 13 ablations show bounded cross-episode ambition persistence in the complete condition and zero persistence without ambition. Human teaching resolved justified help in the complete condition, while the no-teaching ablation left those requests unresolved. These are inspectable symbolic mechanisms, not claims of general intelligence.

## Scene 4 — Narrow specialist rejection (32 seconds)

Week 11's incubated angular specialist achieved fifty-two successes in fifty-two narrow evaluation cases. Week 12 then tested broader transfer: zero successes in thirty-two cases, although all thirty-two were oracle-solvable. The correct result was not promotion. The specialist was rejected and rolled back.

## Scene 5 — Rollback checkpoint (22 seconds)

The authoritative checkpoint is `dde86c0f167653e54db4db74b477fa271b1ee8a2b09e452d0aa622f80d049093`. It contains the general controller only. ## Scene 6 — Week 13 claim status (26 seconds)

Four bounded claims are supported: the reusable rollback skill beats simple baselines on the familiar suite; ambition creates persistent symbolic commitment; human apprenticeship resolves justified help; and retention-gated governance prevents unsupported promotion. C5, broad angular transfer, is explicitly unsupported.

## Scene 7 — Limitations and close (20 seconds)

The rollback controller succeeded in thirty-six of one hundred familiar Week 13 episodes and fifteen of forty broader ball-retention cases. The environment is a small deterministic symbolic Nursery, not robotics or open-world deployment. Week 14 packages the evidence honestly; it does not claim general intelligence, robust real-world manipulation, or successful production specialist growth.
