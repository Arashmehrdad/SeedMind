# ADR: Corrected SeedMind Week 10 Grounded Capacity Diagnosis Closure

Date: 1 July 2026
Status: Accepted

## Context

Original Week 10 must distinguish temporary failure from sustained
developmental blockage before any capacity growth is considered. Failure alone
is insufficient. Week 10 must diagnose and propose only.

Commit `13140df` implemented useful Week 10 structure, but its closure evidence
was invalid because temporary-failure and sustained-blockage timelines, replay
effects, demonstration outcomes, prediction values, and proposal generation were
scripted rather than derived from executed Nursery episodes.

The original `13140df` Week 10 evidence used scripted diagnostic timelines and
was not valid grounded evidence. The corrected implementation derives attempts,
progress, replay outcomes, demonstration effects, prediction evidence,
classification, and proposal generation from executed Nursery episodes.

## Decision

Accept corrected Week 10 closure only through grounded evidence generated by the
main `seedmind.growth` implementation.

The corrected implementation:

1. Executes deterministic Nursery episodes for every Week 10 learning attempt.
2. Records primitive actions, real transition outcomes, state digests, progress,
   success, prediction comparisons, and trace digests.
3. Builds progress windows only from grounded attempts.
4. Persists replay source events through the main `EpisodicSQLiteStore`.
5. Retrieves replay events that resolve to committed source episodes.
6. Measures learner performance before and after protected teacher
   demonstration.
7. Proves sustained-task reachability through an isolated teacher trace.
8. Generates the growth proposal only from complete evidence.
9. Preserves Week 8 familiar skill competence and hash.
10. Creates no specialist, router, parameters, production mutation, or Week 11
    implementation.

## Consequences

- Week 10 is closed by grounded Nursery evidence, not scripted diagnostic rows.
- Original Week 11 specialist growth is the next main-roadmap stage and remains
  not started.
- NDNRA remains frozen at source baseline
  `b9a2ae678938ae1d3dc5e5f4568714dd070a6e2a`.
- No future SeedMind milestone may require NDNRA comparison, promotion, training,
  or integration evidence.
- The Week 10 proposal is a request for later investigation, not authority to
  create or deploy a specialist.
