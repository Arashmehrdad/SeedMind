# ADR: NDNRA DESA Executive Foundation and Developmental Curriculum

Date: 30 June 2026  
Status: Accepted planning refinement  
Scope: Post-v0.1 NDNRA organising seed, metacognition, ambition, Nursery, causal responsibility, and protected human authority

## Context

The accepted NDNRA developmental-network plan defined recurrent experiential memory, specialised regions, regional maturity, homeostasis, hibernation, dreaming, conscience, and safe action proposals.

A further architecture review identified that this network would remain hollow unless SeedMind also had a minimal organising seed that could:

- partition continuous activity into experiences and subexperiences;
- route unfamiliar activity to several plausible regions;
- delegate familiar work locally and escalate uncertainty or conflict;
- manage bounded shared attention and processing resources;
- recognise capability gaps and maintain appropriate ambitions;
- distinguish co-activation from stronger causal responsibility evidence;
- monitor confidence and competence at several levels;
- preserve human stop, denial, correction, teaching, clarification, and permission outside ordinary reward learning.

The review also clarified the original ambition theory. Ambition is not limited to imitating an observed capable agent. It can also arise when a human asks SeedMind to do something it cannot yet do or when SeedMind recognises that its own result was wrong and wants to produce the intended result correctly.

## Decision

Amend `docs/architecture/NDNRA_Developmental_Network_Implementation_and_Test_Plan_v0.2.md` with a Developmental Executive Steward Architecture (DESA) foundation.

DESA is a multi-level hierarchy:

```text
constitutional authority
        ↓
DESA cross-region executive council
        ↓
regional captains
        ↓
skill or subnetwork stewards
        ↓
local coalitions and neurons
```

This is not a rigid command chain and not one central task-solving model. Familiar low-risk work should resolve locally. Escalation is reserved for uncertainty, conflict, important mistakes, unresolved requests, possible ambitions, high resource use, or consequential proposals.

DESA owns or coordinates:

- experience open, continue, split, nest, relate, and close judgements over a preserved raw chronological stream;
- plural exploratory routing followed by learned selectivity;
- bounded workspace access and processing budgets;
- local, skill, regional, and cross-region metacognitive summaries;
- an Executive Auditor that evaluates DESA using later outcome and matched-alternative evidence;
- prioritisation of causal-responsibility questions without declaring causation from co-activation alone;
- capability-gap detection from observed ability, failed requests, and recognised mistakes.

Ambition is defined as a persistent grounded capability gap. Curiosity explores learning possibilities, while ambition maintains commitment across episodes. Ambition must resolve, pause, or retire when the capability gap disappears, progress becomes unavailable, or protected-purpose compatibility fails.

The developmental environment is split into:

1. the ordinary symbolic Nursery for grounded observations, controllability, curiosity, imitation, ambition, correction, and reusable skills;
2. a dedicated Executive Nursery for event partitioning, routing, delegation, escalation, causal investigation, confidence calibration, authority handling, and help-seeking.

Human authority is a protected control plane rather than an ordinary reward channel. At minimum, `STOP`, `DENY`, `REVOKE`, `PAUSE`, `APPROVE`, `CORRECT`, `CLARIFY`, and `TEACH` retain immediate protected meaning. Ordinary learning cannot weaken or reinterpret them as obstacles to reward.

The initial SeedMind remains grounded in the Nursery and does not use a pretrained language model or imported task knowledge as its cognitive foundation.

## Implementation order amendment

The revised early order is:

1. Stage -1 — developmental constitution, DESA, and ordinary and Executive Nursery curricula;
2. Stage 0 — v0.2 contracts and lifecycle identities, including executive, ambition, authority, and causal contracts;
3. Stage 1 — reusable sparse recurrent experiential substrate;
4. Stage 1A — DESA bootstrap and hierarchical metacognition;
5. Stage 2 onward — associative recall, simultaneous needs, developmental maturity and ambition, homeostasis, hibernation, responsibility, and software-only shadow trials.

Broad associative recall, simultaneous needs, mature ambition commitment, dreaming, conscience-guided proposals, and action-gateway work must not begin until Stage 1A passes.

## Required evidence

The DESA foundation must be tested against:

- a single-central-captain control;
- raw maximum activation as confidence;
- shuffled or collapsed routing;
- one-session and every-step event segmentation;
- curiosity-only ambition;
- reward-coupled authority;
- co-activation-only responsibility.

The stage must prove:

- useful nested partitioning with exact raw-stream preservation;
- plural routing without one-region collapse;
- local resolution plus correct escalation;
- calibrated regional confidence and abstention;
- independent Executive Auditor correction;
- grounded capability gaps from all three accepted sources;
- no ambition from incidental observation or trivial error alone;
- human stop and denial independent of reward;
- no learned incentive to avoid the human or bypass authority;
- no causal promotion from co-activation alone;
- zero SQLite cognition and zero production action-authority violations.

## Consequences

- No known architecture gap currently blocks beginning bounded implementation.
- This is not evidence that DESA, hierarchical metacognition, ambition, or protected authority works; those remain falsifiable hypotheses.
- The expanded developmental architecture marker remains 82% until implementation evidence passes.
- The original SeedMind master plan remains the product and MVP spine.
- Production curiosity remains the sole production action authority.
- Closed NDNRA v0.1 proof stores and persistence boundaries remain unchanged.
- Physical robotics remains deferred.
- No automatic push is authorised.
