# ADR: NDNRA Developmental Network Stage 8 Closure

Date: 30 June 2026
Status: Accepted implementation evidence after final gates
Scope: NDNRA v0.2 Stage 8 end-to-end software-only shadow trial

## Context

Stages -1 through 7 established the developmental constitution, schema-separated identities, recurrent experiential substrate, DESA bootstrap, associative recall, specialised concurrent regions, regional and skill maturation, homeostasis, hibernation and restart identity, protected conscience, learned responsibility, and typed non-executable action proposals.

The final authorised research boundary was Stage 8: combine those mechanisms in deterministic software-only shadow tasks while proving that scripted baseline actions remain unchanged and NDNRA obtains no execution authority.

## Decision

Accept Stage 8 as implemented and evidenced by:

- `src/seedmind/research/ndnra/developmental_shadow_trial.py`;
- `tests/unit/test_ndnra_developmental_shadow_trial.py`;
- `docs/architecture/NDNRA_Developmental_Stage_8_Evidence_2026-06-30.md`.

The accepted Stage 8 boundary is:

- deterministic and software-only;
- able to cover six declared shadow task families;
- explicit that actual observed actions remain identical to scripted baselines;
- able to produce context-sensitive, recurrently supported, non-executable proposals;
- able to partition, delegate, escalate, inhibit, and preserve inspectable regional contributions;
- able to preserve multiple simultaneous needs and protected prohibition evidence;
- able to preserve a learned skill bundle, verifier, calibration history, and identity through maturation, dormancy, dream maintenance, and restart;
- able to represent desired-state ambition separately from decreasing capability-gap evidence;
- able to incubate an unfamiliar temporary skill while preserving an unverified outcome when feedback is unavailable;
- within declared retention and local-resource thresholds;
- fully inspectable through typed deterministic evidence;
- non-SQLite;
- zero external side effects;
- zero production action-authority violations;
- disconnected from production runtime adapters and action gateways.

## Validation Decision

The direct full-suite command exceeded CodexBridge's fixed 120-second command limit after reaching 84% without failures. Complete test-file coverage was therefore validated through three non-overlapping groups:

```text
525 passed
355 passed
232 passed
1112 total passed
```

Ruff formatting, Ruff lint, strict Mypy, pip check, and Git diff checks also passed.

## Consequences

- The authorised NDNRA Developmental Network v0.2 research programme is closed through Stage 8.
- Stage 8 does not establish live runtime integration, internet knowledge acquisition, production readiness, or authority eligibility.
- Stage 9 remains unauthorised and must not begin without a separate accepted ADR and explicit human approval defining one narrow reversible action, sandbox, verification, rollback, and failure response.
- Production curiosity remains the sole production action authority.
- Closed NDNRA v0.1 proof stores and persistence boundaries remain unchanged.
- The expanded developmental architecture marker remains 82% because this is bounded research evidence rather than production integration evidence.
- The original SeedMind master plan and Week 8 reusable-skill objective remain mandatory.
- No automatic push is authorised.
