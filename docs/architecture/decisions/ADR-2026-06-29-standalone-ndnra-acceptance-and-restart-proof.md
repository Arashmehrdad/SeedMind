# ADR: Standalone NDNRA Acceptance and Restart Proof

Date: 29 June 2026
Status: accepted
Scope: standalone NDNRA research architecture

## Context

The first NDNRA prototype criteria and many expanded capabilities are already implemented and independently evidenced. Proof remains fragmented across separate experiments, stage closures, and checkpoint boundaries. NDNRA must be completed and proven in isolation before comparison with or integration into the original SeedMind roadmap.

## Decision

The next NDNRA stage is `Standalone NDNRA Acceptance and Restart Proof`.

It will aggregate existing accepted evidence into one deterministic research acceptance boundary covering:

- local delayed credit, need-driven recall, dormancy, and recall cost;
- sparse multi-effect reuse and bounded composition;
- evidence-driven structural growth against equal random capacity;
- restart-safe structural and behavioural state required by the acceptance gate;
- static evidence that bounded imagination remains closed, in-memory, non-evidentiary, and non-authoritative.

The stage must preserve each component report rather than replacing evidence with one opaque aggregate score.

The stage may add a checksum-protected standalone checkpoint only for state required by the acceptance proof. It must not persist bounded-imagination state or strengthen replayed, restored, transferred, or imagined activity into factual evidence.

## Authority boundary

The stage grants no:

- action-selection authority;
- production-action authority;
- recommendation authority;
- scheduling or execution authority;
- autonomous proposal promotion;
- live Nursery integration;
- SQLite cognition.

## Consequences

- The original SeedMind roadmap remains outside the active implementation scope.
- Passing this stage proves consolidated bounded viability and restart equivalence; it does not by itself close every open NDNRA research question.
- Learned similarity, semantic abstraction, simultaneous-need coordination, recruitment normalization, locally derived saturation, growth initialization, and long-horizon interference remain subject to a later explicit retain-or-descope audit.
- The expanded developmental architecture marker remains 82% until new architectural capability, not merely aggregate proof, earns reassessment.
